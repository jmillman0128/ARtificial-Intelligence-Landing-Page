Core Commitment: ARtificial Intelligence is an academic research tool. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from any users.

1. Who We Are

ARtificial Intelligence ("we", "our", "us") is an academic research application developed for the Meta Quest 2, 3, and Pro headsets. The app enables researchers and educators to engage with AI-powered NPC characters across a variety of simulated scenarios. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with your use of our application.

2. No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information from users. This applies to all data types, including but not limited to:

  • Voice recordings or transcripts from in-app conversations
  • Room-mapping or spatial data from your physical environment
  • Usage patterns, session logs, or behavioural analytics
  • Device identifiers or location data
  • Any information that could be used to identify an individual user

Voice permissions are requested exclusively to enable real-time speech-to-text input for interacting with AI characters. Audio is processed momentarily to generate a text prompt and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted beyond what is described in Section 4 below.

3. Research Context

ARtificial Intelligence is intended for use by researchers and educators in structured academic settings. As a research tool:

  • No participant data is captured or retained by our application.
  • Researchers are solely responsible for managing any data they independently collect, in accordance with their institution's ethical guidelines and applicable data protection laws (e.g. GDPR, FERPA).
  • We do not facilitate, enable, or support the collection of user data on behalf of any third-party researcher or organisation.

4. Third-Party AI Services — OpenAI & GPTAvatars

ARtificial Intelligence uses GPTAvatars, which is powered by OpenAI's API (ChatGPT), to generate AI character responses. Here is exactly what happens when you speak to a character:

  • Your voice input is converted to text locally on your device.
  • That text prompt — containing only the content of what you said — is sent securely to OpenAI's API over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection.
  • OpenAI returns a generated text response, which is then spoken by the avatar.
  • No personal information (name, account details, location, biometric data, etc.) is included in or attached to these prompts.

Importantly, as a user of OpenAI's API, data submitted via the API is not used by OpenAI to train their models by default, in accordance with OpenAI's API data usage policy. Prompts and responses are not retained by OpenAI beyond the scope of processing your request.

We encourage users to also review GPTAvatars' documentation for further detail on their platform's data handling practices.

5. Meta Platform & Developer Policy Compliance

ARtificial Intelligence is distributed on the Meta Quest platform. We comply fully with Meta's Developer Data Use Policy. In accordance with this policy:

  • We only request platform permissions that are strictly necessary for core app functionality (voice input).
  • We do not use Meta platform data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the in-app experience.
  • We do not transfer Meta user data to data brokers, ad networks, or analytics providers.
  • We do not use Meta platform data to make eligibility decisions relating to employment, credit, insurance, housing, or education.
  • We do not combine Meta user data with information from other sources for tracking or identification purposes.
  • Room-mapping and passthrough data generated by the Meta Quest headset is used solely to power the mixed reality features of the app and is never transmitted or stored externally.
  • Users may revoke app permissions at any time through their Meta account or headset privacy settings.

6. Children's Privacy

ARtificial Intelligence is designed for researchers, educators, and adult users. The app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly interact with or collect information from children. Any use of the application in educational settings involving minors should be supervised by a responsible adult and carried out in compliance with applicable laws.

7. Data Deletion (Meta Policy 6.2 Compliance)

Because ARtificial Intelligence does not collect, store, or retain any User Data, there is no personal data held by us that would require deletion. This design choice means we satisfy Meta's Data Deletion requirements (Policy 6.2) by default. To be explicit:

  • (a) User deletion requests: No personal data is stored by us, so there is nothing to delete upon request. Users can always contact us at jmillman01@hotmail.com to confirm this.
  • (b) Account deletion: ARtificial Intelligence does not operate user accounts. No user-identifiable data exists on our systems at any point.
  • (c) Data no longer necessary: Since no data is retained beyond the immediate scope of a real-time API call, there is no residual data that could persist once a session ends.
  • (d) Deletion requested by Meta or required by law: We have no stored User Data to delete, and we will cooperate fully with any request or legal obligation to confirm this. Our third-party provider GPTAvatars is bound by their own data policies, including OpenAI's API data retention policy, which does not retain prompts beyond the processing of each request.

9. Data Security

Because we do not collect or store personal data, there is no user data at risk on our end. All communication with third-party API services occurs over encrypted HTTPS connections. We maintain responsible development practices to ensure the integrity and security of the application itself.
